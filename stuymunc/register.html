Cancellation Policy

Due to necessary conference preparations, we will not be able to accommodate any modifications to your invoice beyond 5:00pm on Saturday, March 21st. You will be obligated to pay your remaining balance after this date, and we will not be able to fulfill requests to add or drop delegates to your delegation. Please email StuyMUNC directly at stuymunc2020@gmail.com with any requests to modify your delegation, prior to March 21st.

Payment Procedure

We request that all invoices be paid in full by the conference date. We will be accepting checks upon check-in on Saturday, April 4th. Please make your check payable to: Stuyvesant High School, with Model United Nations (StuyMUN) in the memo. We will also accept mailed checks, and request that you have them postmarked by Saturday, March 21st.
